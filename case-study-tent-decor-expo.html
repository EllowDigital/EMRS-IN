Project overview

From challenge to measurable outcomes.

The challenge

  • Manage thousands of visitor registrations without compromising compliance.
  • Provide exhibitors with a frictionless onboarding and asset submission path.
  • Guarantee VIP and crew access tiers with instant pass updates.

Our approach

  • Configured EMRS forms with multilingual support, approval queues, and invite rules.
  • Automated badge art with association branding, QR encoding, and print-ready exports.
  • Deployed on-ground pods with tablet scanners and manual fallback stations.

Impact delivered

  • Eliminated paper-based bottlenecks across entry gates and help desks.
  • Provided leadership with live dashboards covering attendance, dwell time, and hall heatmaps.
  • Captured post-event insights to plan the next edition’s capacity with data.
